Solid Wooden Table

A sturdy, pre-loved wooden table in good condition. Perfect for dining, study, or home office use. Free for pickup in Pulilan.

  • Posted by: Mary Allyson Caba
  • Date Posted: August 5, 2025
  • Location: Barangay Tabon, Pulilan, Bulacan
  • Category: Home & Living
  • Condition: Gently Used
  • Status: Available

This solid mahogany wooden table has served us well and is ready for a new home! It's rectangular, spacious, and ideal for various uses—from family meals to work-from-home setups. Though it has minor surface scratches from regular use, it's still very stable and reliable. The natural wood finish adds a rustic charm to any space. Offered for free—just bring your own transport for pickup. Let's give it a second life!
