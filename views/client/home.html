Why Join Kalat Mo, Yaman Ko?

Kalat Mo, Yaman Ko is a community-driven platform that helps you give away pre-loved items to people who need them. Promote the 3Rs—Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle—while building a circular economy in your own neighborhood

Help reduce waste and landfill pollution

Practice sustainable living

How It Works

04. Earn Points & Badges

About Kalat Mo, Yaman Ko

Kalat Mo, Yaman Ko is more than just a platform—it's a movement towards sustainable living and community building. We believe that one person's unused items can be another's treasure, creating a cycle of giving that benefits both our community and the environment.
